About Us

We are a team of four developers, studying in the 4th semester, in the Computer Science department of KLE Technological University. This idea came into picture as a result of the Intel Ideation Camp that commenced in the month of February, which is where this idea was brewed initially. The idea was pitched and the four of us came together to discuss whether this can be implemented in real-time. As it can be seen, all the efforts have come to fruition and we have started the process that will join all the hands on this campus and lead to “Clean KLE” eventually.
