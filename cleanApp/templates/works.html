How it Works

Every morning, you wake up, you come to college, attend classes etc. The point being, you are present somewhere on this campus. But sometimes you see a sight or smell a foul odour on the campus, for example, garbage lying around or foul smell from the washrooms or leaked pipes. You wish that it were solved at that very moment.

That is possible now. This web app assists you and at the same time you will help in keeping the campus clean. You ask how? All you have to do, is connect to the KLE Tech network, click a picture of any activity that leads to unhygienic surroundings, give a title to the activity and provide the location [or a landmark in the vicinity], express how severe the problem is through the emoticons

(least)
(most)

and explain briefly about what exactly is happening. That’s it!

The Cleanliness & Maintenance or C & M Cell, will get in contact with you, for more information about it and tend to your complaints and try to mend the situation as soon as possible. So let’s join hands in creating a “Clean KLE” campus.



What's it for

This web app has been developed for the assistance of the C & M Cell, and furthermore the students of this campus who face problems related to bad hygiene on a daily basis. The students who study on this campus will be the source of every piece of information to the C & M Cell. Their problems will be heard to, and be tended to in the quickest time possible. So the cooperation of everyone who studies or does any activity on this campus is requested.
